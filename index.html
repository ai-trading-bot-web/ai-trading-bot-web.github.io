How it works

Launch an AI Trading Bot in three practical steps

Start with the market you want to monitor, refine the rule set, then review the route before any wallet action. The app is built for decentralized finance users who want automation without handing over custody.

1

Choose the market

Select the chain, asset pair and wallet context you want the bot to watch. ETH, BTC-linked assets and supported DeFi routes are handled from the app.

2

Define the signal

Turn your idea into rules for entries, exits, slippage tolerance and risk boundaries, with AI assistance helping you tighten the strategy before execution.

3

Review before action

Preview the live quote, route and required wallet approval. The bot can prepare the workflow, but your wallet stays the final control point.

FAQ

AI Trading Bot questions

01

What is an AI Trading Bot?

An AI Trading Bot is software that helps turn market inputs, strategy rules and live route data into a trade workflow. This app focuses on DeFi previews and user-approved wallet actions, not custody.

02

How does the bot decide when to act?

You set the strategy conditions, risk boundaries and preferred assets. The app surfaces signal context and a live preview so you can review the action before continuing.

03

Do I need to deposit funds?

No. The workflow is non-custodial. You use your wallet, review the app output and keep control of any final approval.

04

How long does setup take?

Setup time depends on the strategy, chain and wallet flow. The app shows the required inputs, live quote and confirmation steps before you proceed.

05

Which chains are supported?

The AI Trading Bot is built for supported DeFi networks and assets shown in the app. See the full list on the supported chains page.

06

What does it cost?

Any network costs, app limits or pricing details are shown in the app before you continue. The page does not publish fixed fees because live trading conditions change.
